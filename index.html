WHY IT MATTERS?

In 2014 Yahoo announced that at least 500 million users had their information stolen. The hack is just one of several firms that have been attacked in the last five years, which range from Ashley Madison to Home Hardware.

While Merriam Webster still defines the term "hack" and "hacking" in terms of computer or program modification it's often now associated with nefarious criminal uses such as identity theft, credit card fraud or other criminal actions.
